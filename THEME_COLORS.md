## How to Switch

Edit `src/config/themeConfig.ts` and change:

```typescript
export const ACTIVE_THEME: keyof typeof THEME_PALETTES = 'themeName';
```

Replace `'themeName'` with any of:
- `'matrixGreen'`
- `'cyberpunkPurple'`
- `'oceanBlue'`
- `'sunsetGradient'`
- `'neonMulti'`
- `'slate'`
